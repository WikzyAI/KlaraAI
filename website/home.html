    Frequently asked questions

    Everything you might be wondering before getting started.

    Is my conversation really private?
    Yes. The bot only operates in your Discord DMs — there is no public log, no shared server, no other users seeing your scenes. Memories are tied to your Discord ID and you can wipe them anytime via /memories. We never sell your data and never train AI on your conversations.
    How does the credit system work?
    100 credits = 1 USD. You can buy credits one-shot (Starter, Popular, Pro, Elite) or subscribe monthly (Standard 1600 cr/mo, Premium 3200 cr/mo). Credits never expire. Subscriptions auto-renew until you cancel — and you can cancel anytime from /premium.
    Is the AI really uncensored?
    Yes. KlaraAI uses a custom system prompt + automatic refusal-detection retry that keeps the model fully in-character even on explicit scenes. The bot will never moralize, warn, or break character. The only hard line is illegal content (minors, real-person targeting) — that's non-negotiable, everywhere on the planet.
    Does the bot remember me between sessions?
    Yes — this is one of our core differentiators. When you end a scene with /erp → End Session, an AI summarizer extracts 3-8 durable facts (your name, established kinks, key moments) and saves them. Next time you start a scene with that character, those memories are injected into her system prompt for true continuity. Rare in Discord-native bots.
    Why pay when JanitorAI / Character.ai are free?
    Three reasons: (1) persistent long-term memory across sessions, which most free competitors don't offer; (2) truly uncensored output — no creative-writing filters that pop in mid-scene; (3) Discord-native — no separate app, no signup, the conversation lives where you already are. Free tier of KlaraAI lets you try before paying.
    Can I create my own characters?
    Standard users can create up to 2 custom characters. Premium users get unlimited custom characters with full control over name, description, and personality. Use /erp → Create Character to build one in under a minute.
    Why the age verification?
    KlaraAI generates explicit adult content. Access is strictly reserved to users aged 18+ (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). The age gate is required by law and by every payment processor. By using the service you confirm you are an adult — false declarations are your own legal risk.
    What about refunds?
    Credits are consumed instantly upon purchase, so all sales are final by default — like any digital consumable. Exceptions: proven duplicate charges or proven service failure are refunded. Reach out at the support email in our Terms if you have an issue.
